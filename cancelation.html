Cancellation Policy

Project Cancellations

Clients may request cancellation of a project at any point. However, once the work has commenced, the 50% advance payment is non-refundable due to resource allocation and time investment.

Cancellation Before Project Start

If cancellation is requested before any work has begun, we will initiate a partial refund process after deducting any consultation or preparation fees already incurred.

Missed Deadlines Due to Client Delays

If delays occur due to unresponsiveness or delay in sharing content from the client’s end, cancellation with refund is not applicable.

Cancellation by the Service Provider

If we must cancel a project due to unavoidable circumstances, a full refund will be issued to the client within 7 business days.

Refund Processing

Refunds, where applicable, will be processed via the original mode of payment within 7-10 business days after approval.
